Patron's Column

We invite you to become a Founder Patron of Vrindavan Chandrodaya Mandir (VCM). You will be one of
the few blessed and fortunate souls chosen for this rare privilege. This will be the most sought-after title
once the historic temple comes up in all its grandeur.

Only 333 Founder Patrons will be enrolled in this limited patronship program.

A Worthy Contribution

Your donation will only be the beginning of your spiritual journey. We want you to stay with us in the proximity of this awe-inspiring temple and participate in its daily programs. We want you to experience the happiness of serving Lord Krishna in sadhu sanga.

Your association with Vrindavan Dham will help you advance in Krishna consciousness and earn permanent spiritual assets.

An Intimate Bond for Generations to Come

We want all your family members, including the younger ones, to benefit from the teachings of Lord Sri Krishna, blissful sankirtan programs and delicious prasadam. This association will create an indelible impression of the glories of Krishna heritage on your family.

The foundation of our future generations should be based on spiritual values and culture. As a result, they will be equipped to succeed in their careers and lives with self-confidence and inner strength.

We offer our founder patrons the following benefits as an appreciation for their service to Lord Krishna :

This is a prestigious membership through which you can get access to enlightening spiritual discourses by senior devotees of the movement.

You will receive a formal invitation for seven Mandir Nirman Pujas during the different stages of the construction of the Grand Temple: Bhumi Puja, Mukhya Dwara Puja, Kalasha Sthapana Puja, Garbhagriha Sthapana Puja, Vigraha Sthapana Puja, Shikhara Sthapana Puja and Prana Pratishtha Puja.

We will conduct special pujas in the temple on any seven days of your choice every year.

You will receive a complimentary copy of Vedic Classics with commentary by Srila Prabhupada and spiritual kit for daily sadhana consisting of sanctified japa mala, japa bag and mantra card.

You and your family shall have access to the exclusive Platinum Patron Lounge, a designated area in the temple for personalised sadhu sanga and spiritual enrichment activities. You will also be provided with a Platinum Patron cum Access Card for use inside the temple complex.

Your name will be displayed in the Hall of Devotional Fame and the Vrindavan Chandrodaya Mandir website. You shall be provided with a specially designed photo frame and coffee table book of the Vrindavan Chandrodaya Mandir.
